ABOUT THE PROGRAM

We are excited to offer a web development internship program for students who are passionate about learning and creating. In this program, you will have the opportunity to work on real-world projects for our online education platform. You will collaborate with our team of experienced developers to create and check assignments, quizzes, and presentations. Additionally, you will work on a major and minor project that will showcase your skills.

  • Assist in developing and maintaining web applications
  • Collaborate with the development team to implement new features
  • Optimize website performance and responsiveness
  • Test and debug web applications
